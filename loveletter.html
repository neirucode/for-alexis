My Dearest Alexis

Happy anniversary, my love.
Another year together, and yet,
it feels like our journey has only just begun.
Each day with you is a new chapter in a beautiful story that I cherish with all my heart.

When I think of how far we've come,
I can't help but smile.
From our first moments together to now,
you've filled my life with so much
warmth, laughter, and love.

You've been my constant source of joy,
my rock, and my heart's greatest treasure.

There's something magical about the way we understand eachother without words,
the way we support one another through everything life throws our way.

I love the little things how you laugh, the way your eyes lightup when you're excited,
the way you hold my hand as if nothing in the world matters more than that simple touch.
Every moment spent with you has been a gift,
and I'm so thankful for it all.

As we celebrate this special day,
I want you to know that my love for you only grows stronger with time.
I look forward to all the memories we've yet to make, all the dreams we have yet to chase,
and all the beautiful moments still ahead of us.

Thank you for being you so perfectly and
wonderfully you.
Cheers to this anniversary, and to many more.
I love you more than words can ever express,
and I'm so incredibly
lucky to share this life with you.

Forever yours,
Ralph Roger
